How fulfilment works

From the moment you send a product link to the moment your customer gets their order.

01

Send us your product

Send us the link — AliExpress, Alibaba, 1688, or just a photo. Tell us your target country, estimated daily orders, and any requirements: colour, size, bundle, custom packaging. We check supplier availability, variant stock, MOQ, domestic lead time, and the best shipping routes for your destination. If we can't find the exact product, we'll tell you honestly and suggest the closest alternative.

02

Approve the quote and connect your store

You receive an all-in price per unit: product cost plus shipping in one number, with carrier, ETA, and available variants included. No hidden fees. Standard dropshipping has no MOQ. Want to compare standard vs express? Just ask. Samples are available on request if you want to verify quality before committing. Once you're ready, Shopify stores connect through collaborator access — no staff account needed. For WooCommerce and other platforms, CSV or Excel order exports work while we set up the integration.

03

We fulfil, track, and help you scale

When orders come in, we source from the factory or pull from pre-stocked inventory, QC at our Guangzhou warehouse, and pack in plain packaging or with your branding inserts. Tracking uploads directly to your store — your customer gets a shipping notification automatically. As volume grows, we support you with inventory storage, faster routes, custom packaging, backup suppliers, and regular performance reporting. The team handling your first order is the same team handling your first thousand.

What if something goes wrong?

Issues happen in fulfilment. Here's exactly how we handle the common ones.

Issue What we do
Package lost by carrier Once carrier loss is confirmed, we arrange a reshipment at no extra product cost.
Item delivered damaged With photo proof, we arrange a replacement for damaged items.
Wrong item shipped We verify the issue and arrange the correct replacement.
Address undeliverable We notify you immediately and request an updated address before reshipment.
Customer claims non-delivery (tracking shows delivered) We request proof of delivery from the carrier and help you decide the next step.

All issues are handled directly by our team. You forward the problem, we resolve it. You do not need to deal with the factory or carrier yourself.
