Quality & Issues

Yes. We run a basic quality inspection on every order before it ships. If we spot a defect or packaging issue, we pull the item before it goes out.

For high-volume sellers or products with a higher defect risk, we can arrange more thorough QC checks. Ask us for details when you get a quote.

Message us on WhatsApp with the order details and photos. We investigate and, where the issue is on our side, we reship or issue a credit. We do not ask you to chase the factory yourself.

Branding & Scaling

Yes. We offer a full range of custom branding options including branded boxes, poly mailers, thank-you cards, inserts, stickers, and product labels. There is usually a minimum quantity for custom-printed items. We quote this separately based on what you need.

Yes. Once you have a proven product, we can pre-stock inventory at our warehouse so orders ship faster. This also helps you avoid stockout risk during peak periods. Ask us about warehousing when you are ready to scale.

Import duties, taxes, and VAT are the responsibility of the recipient — either you or your end customer, depending on your shipping terms and the destination country. We do not collect or pre-pay import taxes on your behalf.

For most standard dropshipping routes (US, UK, Australia, Canada, EU), low-value orders commonly clear customs without additional charges, but this depends on local thresholds and regulations. If your customers are in a market with strict VAT requirements (for example, the EU IOSS threshold), it is worth checking your obligations before you scale.

If you have questions about a specific country, message us — we can share what we typically see on that route, though we are not a customs authority and cannot give formal tax advice.

For confirmed defects, wrong items shipped, or covered lost parcels, we arrange a reshipment or issue a credit — you do not need to organise a physical return.

Physical returns (customer sending the item back to China) are handled case by case. In most situations, the return shipping cost exceeds the product value, so a replacement or credit is more practical. If a return is warranted — for example on a high-value item — we discuss it with you directly and coordinate with the factory.

We do not accept returns for buyer's remorse or change-of-mind orders. Your customer-facing return policy is separate and is your responsibility to set and communicate.
