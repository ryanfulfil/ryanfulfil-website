Order Fulfilment

Move from supplier chasing to a managed fulfilment workflow.

Orders come in from your store, we fulfil them, tracking uploads automatically. What's included in every fulfilment:

  • Product purchase from vetted factory
  • Goods received and inspected at our Guangzhou warehouse
  • Packed and dispatched to your customer's address
  • Tracking number uploaded to your store
  • Shipping confirmation triggered to your customer
No minimum order quantity. One unit ships the same way as one thousand.

Custom Branding & Packaging

Make fulfilment part of your brand experience.

Your customer should experience your brand, not a factory from Guangzhou. We offer branding at every level, from a simple plain package to a fully custom unboxing experience.

Branding option Details
Plain packaging No AliExpress logos, no Chinese writing. Clean and neutral. Default for all orders.
Thank-you cards Custom cards inserted in every order. PDF or PSD artwork from you. MOQ approx. 500 units.
Logo stickers Applied to product or packaging. MOQ approx. 500 units.
Custom box / mailer Branded outer packaging. MOQ varies, typically 500 to 1,000 units.
Product customisation Logo printed or engraved on product. MOQ varies by product type.
Bundle packs Combine products into one branded package: kit, gift set, or upsell bundle.
For artwork, submit PDF, PSD, AI, or EPS files. For early-stage sellers: start with plain packaging and thank-you cards before investing in custom boxes. We'll let you know when your volume justifies the MOQ.
Inventory Warehousing

Reduce delays when your product becomes consistent.

Pre-stock products at our Guangzhou warehouse to skip the 2-3 working day factory-to-warehouse lead time on every order. When goods are already on shelf, we pick, pack, and dispatch the same day.

  • Best for products with consistent daily order volume
  • Reduces delivery time and avoids stock-out risk during Q4 and peak seasons
  • Storage is free while you're receiving active orders from us
  • US, UK, EU, and Japan warehouse partners available for faster local delivery
Ask us to assess whether pre-stocking makes sense for your current order volume. We'll give you an honest answer.

Without pre-stock vs with pre-stock

Without pre-stock

Factory collects order, ships to our warehouse (2-3 days), then we dispatch. Total processing time 3-4 working days before shipping begins.

With pre-stock

Goods already on shelf. We pick, QC, pack, and dispatch same day. Shipping begins within 24 hours of your order coming in.

After-Sales Support

Get practical support when orders go wrong.

We handle complaints and issues with the factory and carrier on your behalf. You forward the problem, we solve it. You do not deal with the factory or carrier yourself.

  • Lost packages: we reship at no extra product cost
  • Damaged items: customer sends a photo, we resend
  • Wrong item shipped: factory confirmed, correct item resent
  • Delivery exceptions: we chase carriers for proof of delivery
  • Return management: discussed case by case

How it works in practice

You forward the issue

Screenshot from your customer is enough. Send it to us on WhatsApp.

We investigate

We contact the factory or carrier directly. You don't need to be involved.

We resolve it

Reship, resend, or refund. We update you with the outcome.

What we don't cover

We're a fulfilment partner, not a turnkey e-commerce agency. We don't build your store, run your ads, or manage your customer service conversations. We handle everything between product sourcing and your customer's door.
