Privacy Policy

How RyanFulfil collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who we are

RyanFulfil is a China-based dropshipping fulfilment service operated by Ryan ("we", "us", "our"). Our website is ryanfulfil.github.io/ryanfulfil-website. We provide product sourcing, packing, and direct-to-customer shipping services for e-commerce sellers worldwide.

2. What information we collect

We collect information you provide directly when you contact us, including:

  • Your name and business name
  • Contact details (WhatsApp number, email address)
  • Product links, order details, and shipping addresses you share with us
  • Messages and communications sent via WhatsApp or our contact form

We do not collect payment card information. Payments are handled through agreed methods directly.

3. How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders
  • Source products and provide quotes
  • Communicate order updates, tracking numbers, and issue resolutions
  • Respond to your enquiries

We do not use your information for marketing without your consent, and we do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Product confidentiality

We understand that your product choices are commercially sensitive. We do not share information about the products you source or sell with any other sellers or third parties. Your winning products stay confidential.

5. How we share your information

We share limited information only where necessary to fulfil your orders:

  • Suppliers and factories — product specifications and quantities required for sourcing
  • Shipping carriers — recipient name, address, and package details required for delivery

We do not share your contact details or business information with suppliers or carriers beyond what is operationally required.

6. Data retention

We retain order and communication records for as long as necessary to support your account and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us on WhatsApp.

7. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We use Google Fonts (loaded from Google's servers), which may set cookies according to Google's own privacy policy.

8. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are based in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to how we use your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via WhatsApp at +86 178 4666 9989.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us. Communications via WhatsApp are end-to-end encrypted. We do not store sensitive payment information.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us on WhatsApp: +86 178 4666 9989.
